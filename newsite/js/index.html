Our Story

The goal of Breakthrough Dressage is to provide Kind, Correct and Effective Training and Lessons for Horses and Riders of All Levels and Abilities.

The heart of our program is USDF Bronze & Silver Medalist Jessie Myers. Jessie is the exclusive trainer for Bronze Mare Farm, a beautiful 90-acre facility in south eastern Michigan, just southwest of Ann Arbor. Jessie has been training horses and riders since 1999.

She believes that dressage is a training method with proven benefits for any horse and rider combination. Jessie is a proven and patient teacher who believes learning takes place only when horse and rider are happy and confident. She applies a systematic approach to dressage training based on the German training pyramid that prioritizes establishing rhythm, suppleness, contact, impulsion, straightness and collection, in that order.

Whether your desire is to compete successfully in recognized USDF shows or simply to grow in partnership with your horse, Jessie can design a program to help you reach your goals.
