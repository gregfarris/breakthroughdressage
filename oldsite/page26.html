Waterloo Summer Dressage Schooling Show
August 5, 2005
Bella, Blue, Jesse and Snickers
Unfortunately rain, rain and more rain made photos impossible, but the horses and riders were wonderful.  Five-year-old Bella returned to the show arena for the first time this year and grew gills!  She and Jessie rode two First Level classes and won them both, each by a margin of nearly 10 percentage points!  Likewise, Jessie and Snickers won both Second Level classes they entered and were named Second Level Champion, Open Division. Jesse and his owner, Tammy Kelly, won third and fourth place ribbons in Training Level Novice Horse with a high score of 62 percent, and Blue and owner Deb Osborne, who also rode Training Level Novice Horse, came away with a Fifth place ribbon.   Despite the horrid weather, it was a great schooling experience and the horses and riders performed beautifully.
