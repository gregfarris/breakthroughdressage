Spring Dressage at Copper Creek
May 19-20
Irish and Vesto

Lorre Hansen and her handsome 8-year-old Irish Draft gelding, aptly named I'm Irish showed Training Level Tests 3 and 4.  It was Lorre's first show in several years and she and Irish performed wonderfully, placing first and second with scores of 68.8 percent and 66.4 percent. 



Below, Jessie and her wonderful new Dutch Warmblood gelding Vesto made their debut at Training Level Open Division Tests 1 and 2, placing second and third with scores of 68 and 66 percent.

June 15 - 17
Irish and Vesto

While proud owner Lorre Hansen looked on,  Yr Johannsdottir earned her first qualifier of the year at Training Level aboard I'm Irish, placing 3rd in a class of 14 with a score of 66 percent.
