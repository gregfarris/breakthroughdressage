Faclity

Bronze Mare farm is a beautiful facility located on 90 acres in Saline Township. We offer quality, full service boarding and training packages for you and your dressage partner.

The main barn features
  • 12X12 stalls with dutch doors overlooking pastures.
  • Heated tack room and restroom facilities.
  • Bright, spacious wash rack.
  • Up to four feedings per day
The arena features:
  • Well insulated indoor with 80 feet of mirrors.
  • Excellent footing.
  • New regulation size outdoor arena
For your horses pleasure:
  • Pasture turnout, all vinyl fencing.
  • Hay fields and wooded trail for cooling / hacking out.
